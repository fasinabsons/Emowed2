# EMOWED - DESIGN SYSTEM & THEME SPECIFICATION

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Design Philosophy:** Modern, Romantic, Trust-Building, Family-Friendly
**Primary Audience:** Indian millennials (25-40), urban, English-speaking
**Brand Personality:** Warm, Professional, Celebratory, Trustworthy
**Accessibility:** WCAG 2.1 AA compliant
