# PAGE SPECIFICATION: HOMEPAGE

## PAGE DETAILS
- **URL:** `/` or `/home`
- **Access:** Public (non-authenticated)
- **Purpose:** Convert visitors into sign-ups
- **Target Audience:** Indian millennials (25-35) looking for relationships/weddings
- **Success Metrics:** 
  - Bounce rate < 40%
  - Sign-up conversion > 5%
  - Time on page > 45 seconds
