## üí° KEY INSIGHTS FROM FOUNDER

1. **Vendors need value BEFORE they pay**
   - Can't charge vendors from day 1
   - They'll pay once they see real bookings coming through
   - Ease of use is critical for vendor adoption

2. **Couples should be the primary revenue source**
   - More predictable than vendor commissions
   - Can charge upfront for premium features
   - Easier to convert (direct value)

3. **Price estimates were too high**
   - Need more realistic projections
   - Focus on volume, not high ticket prices
   - Freemium to premium conversion is key

4. **Organic growth is essential**
   - Word of mouth from happy couples
   - Vendors join when they see weddings happening
   - Build marketplace liquidity slowly
